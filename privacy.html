Privacy Policy — Game Dev Simulator

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Game Dev Simulator is a single-player VR game developed by Jasper Seed ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to play. Save data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Game Dev Simulator is distributed through the Meta Horizon Store. Meta may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policy as part of the platform's standard operation. We encourage you to review Meta's privacy policy at meta.com/legal/privacy-policy for details on what data they collect.

Children's Privacy

This app is rated for teens aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
